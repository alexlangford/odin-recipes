BBQ Sauce

Description

A very popular BBQ sauce. This recipe has a lot of sweetness and spice, so if you're looking for a classic homemade BBQ sauce that's easy to make, look no further. It is very popular with users of 'ceramic' type smokers and grills, (i.e. Primo, Kamado, Big Green Egg, etc.)

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Brown sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Spices & seasonings
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth
  2. Enjoy!!
